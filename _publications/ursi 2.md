This paper studies throughput-based quality adaptation strategies for Dynamic Adaptive Streaming over HTTP (DASH) in 5G mobile networks. Several algorithmic variants are compared in terms of video quality metrics and chunk overlap, with a focus on end-user Quality of Experience (QoE).
