This work was presented at **EUSIPCO 2022**, one of the leading conferences in the field of **signal processing and wireless communications**.

The presentation showcased joint research carried out within the **ITN WindMill** project, focusing on **user localization using RF sensing** and providing a performance comparison between **Large Intelligent Surfaces (LIS)** and **millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar systems**.

This work was made possible thanks to the collaboration with colleagues and partners from the ITN WindMill network, with special contributions from **Roberto Pereira**, **Dariush Salami**, and **Anay Deshpande**.
