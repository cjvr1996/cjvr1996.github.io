**Program:** Master’s Degree in Big Data Analytics  
**Period:** October 2025 – Present  

This course explores the technological environments and ecosystems that support Big Data solutions. Students gain practical knowledge of distributed computing frameworks, data storage architectures, cloud and edge platforms, and data processing pipelines. The course emphasizes the integration of scalable infrastructures with analytics workflows, covering tools and best practices for deploying, managing, and optimizing Big Data systems in real-world industrial contexts.
